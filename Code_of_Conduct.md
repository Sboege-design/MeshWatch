# 🌐 Code of Conduct

We’re committed to creating a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone working on or using **MeshWatch**.

---

## 💬 Our Standards

We pledge to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism graciously
- Show empathy and kindness toward other community members
- Prioritize privacy and safety in all interactions

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment or abuse (online or offline)
- Discrimination of any kind
- Doxxing or sharing personal information
- Dismissive or toxic behavior in GitHub discussions or PRs

---

## 📣 Reporting

If you see or experience violations, please contact the repository maintainer via GitHub issues or private message.

All reports will be handled confidentially and respectfully.

---

## 🙌 Our Commitment

We will enforce this code of conduct consistently and fairly.

This project exists to serve communities often overlooked or threatened. Let's model the kind of world we want our tools to support.

– MeshWatch Team
